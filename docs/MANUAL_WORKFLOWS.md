
# Manual Workflows

This document breaks down the manual UI workflows for complaint creation and work item processing, organized by Damage Type. Each section contains both New Complaint and Existing Complaint flows.

> **Note:** Flows may differ slightly depending on whether a new complaint is being created or an existing one is present, and may also vary by damage type.

---

## Damage Type: Glass Damage

### New Complaint
1. Click **Add New Complaint**
2. Click **Yes/No** button ("Is vehicle drivable?")
3. Click **Glass Damage** button
4. Click **Windshield Chip** (or other sub-type)
5. On **Additional Info** page, click **Submit**
6. If an **Open Complaint(s)** summary or confirmation page appears, review and click **Next** (or **Continue**) to proceed. *(This step may appear for new complaints or certain damage types.)*
7. On **Mileage** dialog, click **Next**
8. On **OpCodes** page, click **Glass Repair/Replace** button
9. Click **Create Work Item**
10. On **Work Items** page, click **Done**
11. Land back on the **main screen**

### Existing Complaint
1. From the main screen, click the **Workitem** tab
2. Click **Add Work Item**
3. Select an existing complaint from the list
4. Click **Next**
5. On **Mileage** dialog, click **Next**
6. On **OpCodes** page, click **Glass Repair/Replace** button
7. Click **Create Work Item**
8. On **Work Items** page, click **Done**
9. Land back on the **main screen**

## Damage Type: PM (Preventive Maintenance)

### New Complaint
1. Click **Add New Complaint**
2. Click **Yes/No** button ("Is vehicle drivable?")
3. Click **PM** button
4. Select **PM Gas** or **PM Oil**
5. Complete any required Additional Info, then click **Submit**
6. If an **Open Complaint(s)** summary or confirmation page appears, review and click **Next** (or **Continue**) to proceed.
7. On **Mileage** dialog, click **Next**
8. On **OpCodes** page, select the appropriate PM code
